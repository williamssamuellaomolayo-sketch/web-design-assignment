About FASL

General Information

The Football Academy of Sierra Leone (FASL) is the premier institution for developing football talent in Sierra Leone. Established in 2018, we are committed to nurturing young athletes through professional training, education, and character development.

Our mission is to produce well-rounded footballers who can compete at national and international levels while maintaining academic excellence and strong moral values.

Agency Partners

We collaborate with various national and international football organizations, educational institutions, and corporate partners to provide our students with the best opportunities for growth and development.

Our partnerships include technical training programs, scholarship opportunities, and international exposure for our most promising talents.

Agency Personnel

Our team consists of experienced coaches, sports scientists, educators, and administrative staff dedicated to the holistic development of our students.

We maintain a low student-to-coach ratio to ensure personalized attention and tailored development plans for each athlete.

Agency History

Founded in 2018, FASL has quickly become a leading football academy in West Africa. Our graduates have gone on to play for national teams and professional clubs both locally and internationally.

We take pride in our contribution to the development of football in Sierra Leone and our role in shaping the next generation of football stars.

Agency Achievements

Since our establishment, we have achieved numerous milestones including multiple youth league championships, successful player transfers to European clubs, and recognition from international football bodies.

Our most significant achievement remains the positive impact we've had on the lives of hundreds of young athletes through our comprehensive development program.
