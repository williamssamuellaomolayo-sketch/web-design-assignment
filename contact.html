Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Football Academy?

We hold tryouts twice a year. Please contact us for information about the next tryout dates and requirements.

What age groups do you accept?

We accept players from ages 8 to 18 in our youth development programs. Our senior academy is for players aged 16-23.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer scholarships to talented players based on both football ability and financial need. Contact us for more information.

What are your training hours?

Training schedules vary by age group and program. Typically, training sessions are held in the afternoons and on weekends.

Can I visit the academy?

Yes, we welcome visitors. Please contact us in advance to schedule a tour of our facilities.
