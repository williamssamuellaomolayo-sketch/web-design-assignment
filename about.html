About FASL

Developing football talent in Sierra Leone since 2018

General Information

The Football Academy of Sierra Leone (FASL) is the governing body of football in Sierra Leone, operating under Rockefeller PRIQ and affiliated to FIFA. Established in 2018, we are dedicated to developing the next generation of football talent through professional training, education, and character development.

Our academy comprises the national league system and oversees the national football teams, including youth categories and the senior national team. We believe in nurturing well-rounded athletes who excel both on and off the field.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To identify, nurture, and develop talented young footballers in Sierra Leone through comprehensive training programs, quality education, and character building, preparing them for successful careers in football and life.

Our Vision

To become the leading football development institution in West Africa, producing world-class players who represent Sierra Leone with excellence and integrity on the global stage.

Our History

2018 - Foundation

Football Academy of Sierra Leone was established with the goal of revolutionizing youth football development in the country.

2019 - First Graduating Class

Our first cohort of players graduated, with several securing contracts with local professional clubs.

2020 - International Recognition

FASL gained affiliation with FIFA and began international exchange programs with European academies.

2022 - Facility Expansion

We expanded our training facilities with state-of-the-art equipment and added residential accommodations.

2024 - European Transfers

Three of our graduates signed with European clubs, marking a significant milestone for Sierra Leonean football.
